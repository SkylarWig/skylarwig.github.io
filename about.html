About me!

Hi! I'm Skylar (or Skye) and I'm a current MSc Astrophysics Student at the University of Sheffield!
Here is a picture of me next to the 16-inch telescope at the Hicks Observatory in Sheffield itself!

I completed my undergraduate degree at Keele University, and was awarded a Bachelor's of Science in Astrophysics with Forensic Science in 2025.
During my time at Keele I learned about my love for binary star systems, while undertaking an introductory module on these systems, and completing a research project about analysing TESS lightcurves of a set of systems that were flagged as potential candidates for the PLATO mission.
